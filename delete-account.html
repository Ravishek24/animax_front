Account Deletion Request

We understand that you may want to delete your account from Pashupalak. This page provides you with clear instructions on how to request account deletion.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including your profile, listings, orders, and transaction history will be permanently removed from our system.

How to Request Account Deletion

Step-by-Step Process:

  1. Send an Email: Contact us at the email address below
  2. Include Required Information: Provide your registered phone number and email address
  3. Verification: We will verify your identity before processing the deletion
  4. Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation email once your account is deleted

Contact Information

Account Deletion Request

Email: ravishek@gmail.com

Subject Line: Account Deletion Request

Response Time: Within 7 business days

Required Information

Please include the following information in your deletion request:

  • Your registered phone number
  • Your registered email address
  • Reason for account deletion (optional)
  • Confirmation that you understand the deletion is permanent

What Happens After Deletion

Once your account is deleted:

  • All your personal information will be permanently removed
  • Your animal listings will be removed from the marketplace
  • Your order history and transaction records will be deleted
  • You will no longer be able to access your account
  • You can create a new account in the future if needed

Data Retention

We may retain certain information for legal or regulatory purposes, such as:

  • Transaction records required for tax purposes
  • Information required for legal compliance
  • Anonymized data for analytics and service improvement

Alternative Options

Before deleting your account, consider these alternatives:

  • Deactivate Account: Temporarily disable your account instead of permanent deletion
  • Remove Listings: Delete specific animal listings while keeping your account
  • Update Information: Modify your profile information instead of deletion
